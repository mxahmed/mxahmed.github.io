Being an Imposter

June, 09 2018

Let me tell you my story, I'm a 22 year old engineering student who have been trying to learn programming since he was 14, and mostly stumbled a lot along the way. I've always been fascinated by computers, those strange white things -  that's how they looked back then -  and what they can do, but mostly I didn't know a damn thing about how they work, and never thought that one day it would be my career to write software for them.
